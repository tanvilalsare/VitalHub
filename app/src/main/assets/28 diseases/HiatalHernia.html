Hiatal Hernia

Symptoms

Common Symptoms:
  • Heartburn
  • Backward flow of swallowed food or liquids into the mouth (regurgitation)
  • Acid reflux (backflow of stomach acid into the esophagus)
  • Trouble swallowing
  • Chest or abdominal pain
  • Feeling full soon after eating
  • Shortness of breath
  • Vomiting of blood or passing of black stools, indicating possible bleeding in the digestive tract

Treatment

Medication:
  • Antacids
  • Medicines to reduce acid production: cimetidine, famotidine, nizatidine
  • Proton pump inhibitors: lansoprazole, omeprazole

Lifestyle and Home Remedies

  • Eat several smaller meals throughout the day instead of a few large meals.
  • Avoid foods that trigger heartburn: fatty or fried foods, tomato sauce, alcohol, chocolate, mint, garlic, onion, and caffeine.
  • Avoid lying down after a meal or eating late in the day.
  • Maintain a healthy weight.
  • Stop smoking.
  • Elevate the head of your bed by 8 inches (20 centimeters).

Information about Medication

Cimetidine
  • Side effects include: headache, diarrhea
Famotidine
  • Side effects include: headache, dizziness, constipation, or diarrhea
Nizatidine
  • Side effects include: headache, dizziness, diarrhea, or runny/stuffy nose
