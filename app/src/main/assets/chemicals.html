Top 8 Toxic Chemicals to Avoid in Skincare

1. Parabens

Preservatives

Used in products like moisturizers and shampoos. Linked to potential breast cancer risks and hormone disruption, though effects vary by individual.

2. Sulfates

Foaming Agents

Commonly found in toothpaste and cleansers. Can strip skinâ€™s moisture, irritate, and pose risks when combined with other substances.

3. Phthalates

Softeners

Found in products like nail polish and hair sprays. Known as endocrine disruptors linked to hormone issues, cancer, and reproductive defects.

4. Fragrance

Aromatic Ingredients

A mix of synthetic and natural ingredients. Can irritate skin, disrupt hormones, and potentially cause cancer.

5. Formaldehyde

Preservative

Used in nail polish, makeup, and lotions. A known carcinogen that can cause skin irritation and respiratory issues.

6. Phenoxyethanol

Antibacterial Agent

Common in cosmetics and perfumes. Can irritate skin, harm the central nervous system, and pose risks to nursing mothers and infants.

7. Alcohols

Harsh Types

Types like methanol and benzyl alcohol can dry skin, strip its barrier, and worsen inflammation over time.

8. PEGs

Thickeners & Absorption Enhancers

Can irritate damaged skin, reduce moisture, and accelerate aging.
